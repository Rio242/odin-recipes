Banana Bread

Description

This is a delicious banana bread recipe made

Ingredients

  • All purpose flour
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Brown Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Bananas

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour, baking soda and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl, cream together butter and brown sugar.
  4. Stir in eggs and mashed bananas until well blended.
  5. Bake in preheated oven for 60 to 65 minutes
